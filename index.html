Skies wide open

Introducing the first complete digital marketplace for private aviation.

For the first time, get hundreds of aircraft options, with incredible accuracy, in seconds.

Rocket science for jets.

The most amazing thing about Stellar is the technology inside. In order for aircraft operators to participate in the 21st century, their technology platform had to be rebuilt from the ground up. Developed by an experienced team of PhDs and computer scientists, the outcome was Stellar CORE—the world’s first commercial operations system for Part 135 business jet operators. Then we connected it to the first all-digital marketplace.

Choose from the best brands in business aviation.

Stellar has the largest selection of platinum-rated aircraft from the best brands and most trusted FAA-certified Part 135 operators in North America.

Search for the ideal aircraft option in seconds.

Gone are the days of waiting days to get three quotes. Stellar scans the real-time availability and pricing directly from operators to give you the best aircraft options in seconds, including one-ways, deadheads, and transient aircraft.

Select the best aircraft for your mission.

Stellar gives you access to the world’s largest digitally connected fleet. Choose from 114 aircraft types, from ultralong-range Gulfstream G650s, to ultrafast Citation Xs, to ultraefficient PC-12s, then filter by age, class, or other criteria.

Get direct pricing from operators.

Stellar’s marketplace removes the pricing guesswork. You always get accurately priced aircraft options directly from operators in seconds. Also choose from capped pricing models with guaranteed availability from our partners.

Pay effortlessly with touch ID.

Stellar built in the first high-value payments network specifically designed for business aviation. Select your preferred payment method, such as directly from your bank, your favorite credit card, or even Apple Pay.

Private aviation now available online.

With Stellar, it’s easier than ever to explore a new world of travel.

Everything you need to search, select, and pay is at your fingertips.
